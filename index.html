How to use:
Step 1: Look in your meta tab and find the 2 values for "density" and "gravity".

Step 2: Look at your current meta, type it in and press submit.

Note: For high values, switch to scientific in settings, and add that value in directly without the "+".
(eg 6.55e+4 means you enter 6.55e4).

Step 3: You should get a result back telling you what to do, simply buy as many upgrades as it specifies.


DISCLAIMER! This Calculator assumes that the starting and ending value for meta is the value you use.
If you want to buy other upgrades, buy them first, then add the updated amount to the calculator.
